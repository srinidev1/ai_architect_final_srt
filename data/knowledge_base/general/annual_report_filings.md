# Delaware Annual Report and Franchise Tax Filings

**Source:** https://corp.delaware.gov/paytaxes/  
**Jurisdiction:** State of Delaware — Division of Corporations  
**Last Verified:** May 2026

---

## Overview

All active Delaware corporations must file an Annual Report and pay Franchise Tax each year. Filing is done exclusively online through the Delaware Division of Corporations portal.

**Filing Portal:** https://icis.corp.delaware.gov/ecorp/logintax.aspx  
**Portal Availability:** Daily, 8:00 AM – 11:45 PM Eastern Time

> **Important:** Nature of Business is now required on all Domestic Annual Reports. This indicates what the company does to generate revenue. Use only English characters when entering data to avoid inaccurate records.

---

## Domestic Corporations — Annual Report & Franchise Tax

### Due Date
Annual Reports and Franchise Taxes for the prior year are **due on or before March 1** each year. Filing must be completed online.

### Penalties for Non-Filing
Failure to file and pay results in:
- **$200.00 penalty**, plus
- **1.5% interest per month** on tax and penalty combined

### Annual Report Filing Fees (effective September 1, 2019)

| Corporation Type | Filing Fee |
|---|---|
| Exempt Domestic Corporations | $25.00 |
| Non-Exempt Domestic Corporations | $50.00 |

---

## Franchise Tax Rates — Domestic Corporations

Taxes apply to any corporation active in Delaware records at any point between **January 1 – December 31** of the current tax year.

| Category | Minimum Tax | Maximum Tax |
|---|---|---|
| Standard (both methods) | $175.00 (Authorized Shares) / $400.00 (Assumed Par Value) | $200,000.00 |
| Large Corporate Filer | — | $250,000.00 |

Corporations use whichever of the two calculation methods results in the **lesser tax**.

## Estimated Tax Payments (Corporations Owing $5,000+)

Corporations owing $5,000.00 or more must make estimated payments on the following schedule:

| Payment | Amount Due | Due Date |
|---|---|---|
| 1st installment | 40% | June 1 |
| 2nd installment | 20% | September 1 |
| 3rd installment | 20% | December 1 |
| Final remainder | Balance | March 1 |

---

## Non-Stock, For-Profit Corporations

Non-stock for-profit corporations that do not qualify as Exempt Corporations must pay a flat Franchise Tax of **$175.00**.

---

## Foreign Corporations — Annual Report

| Item | Details |
|---|---|
| Due Date | On or before **June 30** each year |
| Filing Fee | $125.00 |
| Late Penalty | $125.00 added to filing fee if not received by due date |

## Exempt Corporations

Certain corporations qualify for exempt status with a reduced filing fee of $25.00.  
Reference: https://corp.delaware.gov/exemptcorp/

---

## Large Corporate Filers

Corporations identified as Large Corporate Filers have a maximum franchise tax of **$250,000.00** (vs. the standard $200,000.00 cap).  
Reference: https://corpfiles.delaware.gov/LargeCorporateFiler.pdf

To remove a corporation from Tier 2 (Large Corporate Filer) status, provide the SEC delisting form to Franchise Tax.

## Scam / Fraud Alerts

The Division of Corporations warns of deceptive solicitations. Delaware entities should be suspicious of any correspondence not coming directly from the State or their registered Delaware agent.

- **Report scams:** Complete a complaint form at https://attorneygeneral.delaware.gov/fraud/cmu/complaint/
- **Consumer Protection Unit:** (302) 577-8600 or 1-800-220-5424
- **Division of Corporations:** (302) 739-3073
- **Examples of deceptive solicitations:** https://corp.delaware.gov/consumeralert/
